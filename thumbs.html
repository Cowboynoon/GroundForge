GroundForge

A thumbnail index for matching sets of diagrams.

A single thumbnail may link to several patches that vary in shape. Even a dull Torchon ground may represent interesting variations: by pins at unorthodox positions, by unusual angles on the foot sides (check the links that appear along the patches) and by stitch variations causing threads taking different routes than pairs.
