GroundForge

Droste effect: Use thread diagrams as pair diagrams to generate bobbin lace grounds.

When you follow the linked stitches and show the diagrams, the level 2 diagrams reveal a traditional ground. The left column shows the official color coded diagrams for level 2. The generated diagrams however don't have pins and the pair diagrams draw a plait or something-pin-something like a simple stitch.

The table shows the most interesting stitches. Mirror, mix, add or drop twists and/or try a weaving base pattern. Mixing stitches requires larger dimensions. For your convenience some valid (see the user guide) matrices are predefined with the following links. Diagonal checker: 2x2, 2x4, 4x2, 4x4, bricks: 1x2, 3x2, 2x4. Weaving checker 2x1, 2x2, 2x3, 2x4, 4x1, 4x2, 4x3, 4x4, bricks: 2x2, 2x4, 4x2, 4x4.
