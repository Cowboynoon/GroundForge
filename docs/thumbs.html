GroundForge

A thumbnail index for matching sets of diagrams.

Select a thumbnail to create your own variations on the pattern.

Drag the thumbnails around to order them by your own classification and save the result on your hard disc. The first group of thumbnails link to patterns with several patches that vary in shape or are mirrored versions. The second group has just a single patch per pattern.

Even a basic Torchon ground may represent interesting variations: by pins at unorthodox positions, by unusual angles on the foot sides and by stitch variations causing threads taking different routes than pairs. These patterns are just a tip of the iceberg discovered by TesseLace.com
