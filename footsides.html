GroundForge - select foot sides

N.B: rendering so many thumbnails requires a lot of resources, so may not be suited for tablets.

The left upper thumbnail of each group doesn't shift the matrix rows and columns. Each next row and/or column does shift the matrix to get another foot side. Say you like the foot side of the third thumbnail on the second row, Then you should specify as shift options: rows 2 and columns 1. You can add the width of the matrix to the title of the group of thumbnails you like. Say you like the foot side in the group of thumbnails in the group titled "7 columns" and the matrix is four columns wide, then eleven or fifteen columns are also okay.
